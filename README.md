# Toddler Photo Lock

#### Made by a dad for his kid.

A free iOS app that lets you choose a photo and disable controls for your toddler.

Download in the iOS app store coming soon.

## How it works

1. Select an image from your camera roll
2. Start iOS's [Guided Acces](https://support.apple.com/en-us/111795)
3. Hand your phone to your toddler

No other apps or controls can be accessed until you end Guided Access. It's that simple!

## Privacy

No data or images are uploaded. Toddler Photo Lock is open source and has no tracking. See the [privacy policy](https://github.com/ZachSaucier/Toddler-Photo-Lock/wiki/Privacy-policy)
